Premier Pro Shortcuts

***  Add Keyframe attributes to multiples clips ****

- Select desired clips

- Go to "edit" -> "Paste Attributes"

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Show everything in timeline ****

- Hit backslash ( \ )

------------------------------------------------------------------


***  Make selected section full screen ****

- Hit ( ~ ) key

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Add a default transition hotkey  ****

- CTRL - d

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Change default transition ***

- In "Effects" right click transition and set as default transition

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Delete clip and ripple  ****

- ALT + Backspace

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Ripple Trim Tool  ****

- To delete clip and ripple space at play head location press 'Q'

*** Create gap for clip in Timeline ***

- Grab clip you want you want to insert into the timeline pressing "CTRL" to place in-between clips in desired location

- CTRL + ALT

*** Loop Marked section of clip ***

- In your timeline mark in  - (I) then mark out mark out - (O)

- Click the loop video button 

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Mouse Scroll Timeline  ****

- With timeline selected, hold shift and scroll with mouse wheel to move through timeline

------------------------------------------------------------------

***  Simplify Tracks  ****

- Navigate to "Sequences" in the options bar found at the top then click "Simplify Sequence"
